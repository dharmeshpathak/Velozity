Our Processes

Analysis. Planning Stage. Design.
Development & QA. Launch.
Maintenance and Growth.

Analysis

Our team conduct a research of:


Goals and strategy of the product. Structure and roles within the project. Project needs to define optimal methodology and approach to robust development.

Planning Stage

At this stage we develop:


The detailed structure of the project. A functionality described by user-stories. UX Design. An accurate estimation of the project development.

Design

This step allow us to:


Explore product requirements. Continue the research using the data from analysis stage. Build the prototype. Create design concept. Produce design. Test design to ensure positive user experience.

Development & QA

We tune development process to follow such methodology that cope with the project's environment.


We focus on QA testing during the development to deliver quality products to the customer. We are transparent throughout the process and eager to demonstrate how work's executed.

Launch


At this stage each envolved parties are prepared for the public launch. We deliver product to the end users utilizing CI/CD approach.

Maintenance and Growth

At this stage we focus on:


Technical and customer support. Setting key metrics for critical growth indicators. Using itterative approach to utilize continious growth strategy.
